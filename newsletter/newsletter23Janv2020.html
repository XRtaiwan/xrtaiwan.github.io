Happy New Year all! It's been a bit since we have sent out a newsletter due to the holidays- but now we are back and ready to tackle this new year!

We have a lot of events coming up, and we hope that you can attend.

Recent Actions

We have done two actions already this year. Our first was the day of the election where several rebels went to different locations across the city to raise awareness about the climate crisis.

Great work rebels!

The second action was this past weekend- 'plogging.' Rebels went out around Dahu Park and picked up trash.

Excellent work!
